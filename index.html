Frequently Asked Questions

What is KYC, and why is it required?

KYC, or Know Your Customer, is a verification process to confirm the identity of our users. It's crucial for ensuring a secure and compliant trading environment, preventing fraud, and complying with regulatory requirements. For our presale, undergoing KYC means you're part of a trusted community committed to security and transparency.

What does an audit entail, and why is it important?

An audit, especially for crypto projects, involves a thorough examination of our smart contracts and systems by independent security firms, like Cyberscope. This process identifies vulnerabilities and ensures that our contracts operate as intended, safeguarding participants' investments and reinforcing the integrity of our platform.

How does the presale work?

Our presale is divided into three stages, each offering ONI tokens at a different price point. Early participants benefit from lower prices, with the cost increasing in each subsequent stage. Funds raised will be used to further develop the platform, ensuring a robust launch and future growth.

What happens if I miss the presale?

If you miss the presale, you'll have the opportunity to buy tokens after the official launch, albeit at the market price. Participating in the presale offers the advantage of acquiring tokens at a lower price, so we encourage early participation.

How will the funds raised in the presale be used?

Funds raised will be allocated to key areas such as further development of the ONI platform, liquidity provision for exchanges, marketing to broaden our reach, and operational costs to ensure smooth operations and scalability.

What is the affiliate program, and how does it work?

Our affiliate program rewards you for referring new participants to our presale and NFT sales. As an affiliate, you'll earn a 10% commission in BNB directly to your wallet for every sale made through your referral. Each token sale and NFT sale page has its own affiliate dashboard, where you can track your earnings and manage your referrals.
